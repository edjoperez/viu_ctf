History of Evil Corp

Evil Corp was founded in 2005 as a small technology startup focused on advanced automation software development. Established by Johnathan Blackwood and Sarah O’Connor in a small garage in London, the company began with a simple goal: to revolutionize the way industries operate through technology.

About Evil Corp

Evil Corp is a global leader in technology and innovation. We provide cutting-edge solutions that drive progress and transform industries. Our commitment to excellence and our unwavering focus on the future make us the partner of choice for businesses and governments worldwide.

Mission

“To drive global technological transformation by providing solutions that redefine efficiency, sustainability, and innovation in every industrial sector.”

Vision

“To build an interconnected and intelligent future where technology not only enables progress but also generates a positive impact on communities, facilitating a more efficient, safe, and sustainable world.”
